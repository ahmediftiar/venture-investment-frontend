Key Activities

We founded Venture Investment to aid in the creation of financial equality and opportunity for all. Working with early-stage businesses to play a more active role in assisting founders with common early-stage difficulties such as hiring, strategic focus, valuation policy, and business alliances.

Segmentation

We invest in early-stage technological firms that are on the cusp of a game-changing growth opportunity. Where founders are committed to chasing the value of Health, Education, Agriculture, Environment, Robotics (Nanotech/IoT/AIOT/IIOT), Financial Services, Transportation, Food Processing, Pharmaceutical, Manufacturing, Media & Entertainment, eGovernment/ mGovernment, eCommerce domain in Southeast Asia, Africa and USA Market.

Core Philosophy

We are a venture capital business that invests in entrepreneurs whose discoveries assist individuals in having access to technological applications that allow them to focus on what matters most in life.

Beginning IT

Beginning IT provides low-cost product development services to entrepreneurs in Bangladesh, as well as branding, marketing, and assistance with investment pitch decks.
